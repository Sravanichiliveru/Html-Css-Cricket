Introduction

Small Note

Virat Kohli is an Indian International Cricketer. He was a Former Captain of Indian Cricket team. He was also a former captian of Royal Challengers Banglore(RCB) in IPL. He has made 75 centuries in international cricket.

Family Background

Virat Kohli was born on 5 November 1988 in Delhi to a Punjabi Hindu family. His father, Prem Kohli, worked as a criminal lawyer and his mother, Saroj Kohli, served as a housewife. He has an older brother, Vikas, and an older sister, Bhawna. He married an actress names Anushka Sharma and has a child named Vamika.

Career

Early stage

At the age of 9 years in the year 1998, Virat Kohli joined West Delhi Cricket Academy after his father recognized the potential in him. He was trained under the guidance of Rajkumar Sharma.In the year 2006, Virat Kohli lost his father and his family faced hard time. For the time in 2002, he played for Delhi under 15 Team and secured highest scores in this year. After that he played under 17 team and secured highest rums. He continued his journey playing State matches.

Present Stage

Finally he became Captain after the retirement of M.S.Dhoni. Recently he retired from the captain. He had reached 75 centuries recently. He has become one of the most cricketer to have highest score.
